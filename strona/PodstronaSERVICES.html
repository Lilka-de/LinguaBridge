Services

We offer professional translation services in three languages: Polish, English, and German. We support both individuals and businesses in preparing texts and facilitating communication across different languages and cultures. Every assignment is treated individually, with careful attention to linguistic accuracy, style, and faithful representation of the original meaning.

📄 Written Translations

Written translation involves rendering documents and texts from one language into another while preserving the meaning, style, and terminology. This covers company documents, informational materials, articles, websites, marketing content, as well as official and training documentation.

We also offer proofreading and editing services to ensure the highest linguistic quality and terminological consistency.

🎤 Spoken Translations

Spoken translation involves conveying speech in real time between participants communicating in different languages. It is essential during business meetings, conferences, training sessions, international events, and conversations with foreign clients and partners. It ensures that communication runs smoothly and effectively.

⚖️ Sworn Translations

Our sworn translation services cover both written and spoken translations of documents requiring official certification, such as birth certificates, diplomas, contracts, court documents, and administrative records.

🌍 Areas of Specialisation

  • 🩺 Medical – medical records, academic articles, informational materials
  • ⚖️ Legal – contracts, official documents, terms and regulations
  • ⚙️ Technical – user manuals, technical documentation, product and process descriptions
  • 📊 Business – reports, presentations, corporate correspondence
  • ✈️ Tourism – travel guides, promotional materials, websites
  • 📚 General informational materials – brochures, articles, educational content

All translations are carried out in Polish, English, or German, ensuring reliable and professional service tailored to the needs of each client and the specific requirements of every project.
